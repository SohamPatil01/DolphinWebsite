World-Class Facilities

Our campus is designed to inspire learning, creativity, and holistic development

Our Campus Facilities

Learning Resource Center

A state-of-the-art library with 15,000+ books, digital resources, quiet study areas, and collaborative learning spaces.

  • Digital catalog system
  • Reading nooks and study pods
  • Online research databases

Science & Innovation Labs

Fully equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Robotics with modern equipment and safety features.

  • Dedicated robotics lab
  • 3D printing and prototyping
  • Student research projects

Sports Complex

Comprehensive sports facilities including indoor and outdoor courts, swimming pool, and athletic tracks.

  • Olympic-size swimming pool
  • Basketball, volleyball, badminton courts
  • Football field and running track

Performing Arts Center

Dedicated spaces for music, dance, drama, and visual arts with professional-grade equipment.

  • Auditorium with 500-seat capacity
  • Music practice rooms
  • Art studios and gallery

Technology Center

Modern computer labs with the latest hardware and software, supporting digital literacy and coding education.

  • 1:1 device program for higher grades
  • Programming and AI labs
  • Multimedia production studio

Dining & Wellness

Hygienic cafeteria serving nutritious meals, plus health center with full-time nurse and counseling services.

  • Nutritious meal plans
  • Health clinic with first aid
  • Student counseling center
