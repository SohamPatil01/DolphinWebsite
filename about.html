About Vidyanchal School

Founded in 2005, Vidyanchal School is a premier co-educational institution in Pune dedicated to nurturing responsible global citizens through academic excellence and holistic development.

Our History Meet Our Team

Our Story

Vidyanchal School was established with a vision to create an educational institution that goes beyond textbooks and examinations. Our founders believed in nurturing young minds to become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals ready to face the challenges of the modern world.

Over the years, we've grown from a small school with just 120 students to one of Pune's most respected educational institutions, serving over 1,000 students across all grades. Our commitment to quality education and holistic development has remained unchanged since our inception.

Our Journey

2008

First batch of Class X students achieves 100% pass rate in board exams

2012

Moved to current 5-acre campus with modern infrastructure

2015

Launched STEM program and robotics lab

2018

Recognized as "Best Emerging School" by Education Times

2022

Expanded to include senior secondary classes (XI-XII)

2025

Celebrating 20 years of educational excellence with 1000+ students
